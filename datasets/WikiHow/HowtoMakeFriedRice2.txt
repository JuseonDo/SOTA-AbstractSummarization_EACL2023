@article
 of peanut oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat., Add 2 lightly beaten eggs to the pan. Tilt the pan to coat the surface with the eggs. Cook the eggs in an even layer until they're cooked all the way through. Halfway through the cooking, after about 2 minutes, flip the eggs. Then, remove the eggs, dice them into small pieces, and set them aside.
 Heat 1/2 cup of chopped onion, 3 cloves of minced garlic, and 2 tsp. of minced ginger to the pan. Cook these ingredients together for 2 minutes in the remaining peanut oil.
 The pork chops should already be cooked. Cook the diced pork for an additional 3 minutes, or until they're golden brown.
 Add 1 cup of cooked brown rice, 1/4 cup of reduced-sodium soy sauce, and 2 tsp. of sesame oil to the pan and cook them for 2 more minutes. Season the fried rice to taste with salt and black pepper. Then, remove the fried rice from heat.
 Mix the cilantro well with the remaining ingredients.
 Place this fried rice on a serving plate and top it with chopped eggs.
