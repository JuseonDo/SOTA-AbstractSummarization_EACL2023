 Place any amount of salted or unsalted butter in a pan and melt it completely. Don't overcook it or it will brown.
 The solids will rise to its surface.
 Pour the butter through a clean tea towel or damp cheesecloth after melting. Let the liquid run through the cloth into a bowl.
