 Use a pita bread as the base instead of regular pizza dough. Place the pita bread on a plate.;
, Get the tomato sauce and spread it on the pizza base using a spoon.
 Consider adding mushrooms, tomatoes, peppers, cheese, pepperoni, bacon, olives, garlic powder, etc.


Check out other pizza toppings online. This can give you more ideas and variations to the pizza.
If you want, have a garlic crust. Sprinkle garlic powder on the edges of the pita bread.
 Warm up the pizza for about a minute, until the cheese melts and the toppings slightly soften.
 Remove the pizza from the microwave using cooking mitts. Slice the pizza in triangular slices. Enjoy!
