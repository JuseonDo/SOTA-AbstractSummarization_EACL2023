 Baking soda mixed with warm water is another good way to clean your hair combs from dirt and hair product residue. Pour 1 teaspoon of baking soda into a small amount of water and mix thoroughly. , This will allow the baking soda to activate and clean your hair combs of hair and styling product buildup. Make sure your hair combs are free of dirt and hair before you remove them from the water mixture.
 Use a towel to pat your hair combs dry. This will remove any moisture on the handle and bristles. You can also let it sit overnight to make sure it is thoroughly dry.
