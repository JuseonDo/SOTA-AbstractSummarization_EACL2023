 Then wrap it in a damp tea towel (this keeps the pastry moist, as it tends to dry out quickly)., Trim the edges and brush with melted butter. Keep repeating this process until half of the pastry is used., Cook until all of the ingredients are well blended. Spread the cooked meat mixture evenly on top of the phyllo dough pastry., Spread the remaining melted butter over the top layer.
