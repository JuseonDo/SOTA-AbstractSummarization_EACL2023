candle lamps are useful in outdoor lamp posts, wall sconces, decorative chandeliers, fans and some reading lamps.
@article
 They often come with two base(the part that screws into the socket) sizes. These sizes are called a medium base (larger of the two) and a candelabra base (fits mostly chandeliers and some sconces). Candle lamps provide a warm and inviting soft white light and last 6000 to 8000 hours.
