 Lightly coat a shallow 13x9 inches baking dish with nonstick spray. Add 1/4 cup water to dish. Cut a thin slice off ends of squashes. Cut squashes in half crosswise; remember to scrape out the seeds with a spoon. Arrange in dish, and cut the sides up. Cover it tightly with some foil.
