, Sieve the flour to let in air. Rub in the cubed butter until it resembles breadcrumbs.;
, Add the eggs, caster sugar and milk. Mix evenly, then beat.
 Mix to combine gently. It should form a smooth dough.
 Place about 1/2 of the bar into a bowl suspended over a boiling pot of hot water. Allow the chocolate to melt slowly, stirring the chocolate. Don't let the chocolate burn. Once the chocolate has melted, add the 4 tablespoons of drinking chocolate, stir gently.
 Stir through the chocolate sauce. Once combined, remove from the heat and let stand for 5 minutes.
 Gently mix it together, making sure that the chocolate orange flavour chocolate is evenly mixed on through the pastry mix.
, Flour the board and rolling pin. Roll out to an even thickness. Place parchment/baking paper beneath the pastry.
, Use the ends of the parchment paper to tightly roll the pastry dough and hold it in a roll shape. It does not matter if pastry cracks.
 Turn down heat to 120ºC, and bake for 20-25 minutes.
, When cooled, cut the log cake into equally shaped squares and dust with icing sugar.
