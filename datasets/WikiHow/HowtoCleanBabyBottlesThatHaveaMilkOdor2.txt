make a solution of one part white vinegar and three parts warm water.

@summary
ensure all parts of the bottle are soaked and submerged in solution.

@summary
let the bottle and its parts soak in the vinegar solution overnight.
@article
 Then, fill the bottle with the solution. You can also fill a container large enough to fit the bottle and all its parts with the solution., You can do this by pressing the the parts down with a heavy object like a plate.
 In the morning, rinse the bottle and all its parts with water and detergent.


The smell of vinegar may be present for a few minutes, but it will quickly evaporate.
