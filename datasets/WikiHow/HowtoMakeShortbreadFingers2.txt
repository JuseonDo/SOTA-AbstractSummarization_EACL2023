@article
 You'll need:

1 1/2 cups or 340g or 3 sticks unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
2 1/2 cups + 2 tablespoons (29.6 ml) or 370g all-purpose or plain flour
4 1/2 tablespoons cocoa powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
Pinch salt
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup or 200g superfine or caster sugar
Granulated sugar, for garnish.
 Either grease it or line with parchment paper.,, Beat together on medium speed, until light and fluffy., Mix to combine but do not overmix., Lay another layer of parchment paper over the top. Then press down across the dough to spread it across the sheet or pan evenly., Leave for 15 to 25 minutes. About 10 minutes before removing the dough, preheat the oven to 160ºC/325ºF., Remove the sheet of paper. Using a fork, prick holes across the dough., Bake until firm, approximately 20 to 35 minutes., While still warm, cut into finger slices. Sprinkle all of the fingers with granulated sugar.,, Once cooled, the shortbread fingers are ready to eat. If storing, keep in an airtight container lined with greaseproof paper.
