@article
 Take care to remove an equivalent tablespoon of flour from the amount required by the ingredients. The easiest way to do this is to fill a cup of flour on top of the tablespoon of cornflour and count that as one cup of flour. The ratio should be one tablespoon per cup of flour required.;
, Bake as recommended.
 You should find that the addition of the cornflour prevents the roll from cracking or breaking, making it much easier to roll.
