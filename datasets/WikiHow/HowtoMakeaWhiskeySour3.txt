 Shake 0.75 oz. (22 ml.) of whiskey, 0.75 oz. (22 ml.) of gin, 0.75 oz. (22 ml.) of lemon juice, 0.5 oz. (15 ml.) of simple syrup, and 1 dash of grenadine together for at least ten seconds to blend the flavors.
 Strain the ingredients into a sour glass or an old fashioned glass filled with ice.
 Garnish this drink with a maraschino cherry and an orange slice and enjoy it immediately.
