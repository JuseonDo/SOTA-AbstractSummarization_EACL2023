 This is another easy option if you want to universally enable JavaScript on all websites, but are intimidated by about:config. This add-on will allow you to enable or disable JavaScript in the “about:preference” menu. You can find it here, or search “SettingSanity” on addons.mozilla.org. Click, “Add to Firefox," then “Install.” You will have to restart your browser.
, Under “Images and JavaScript," you’ll see “Enable JavaScript.” Click the checkbox next to it so that it’s filled. JavaScript is now enabled on your browser.
