@article
 Here's what you'll need to make baked tilapia:


4 tilapia fillets
1 tbsp. melted butter
1 tbsp. olive oil
1 juiced fresh lemon
2 cloves minced garlic
Sea salt to taste
Black pepper to taste
2 tbsp. chopped parsley;
,, Alternately, you can lightly brush the dish with olive oil.
 Pat the fish dry between layers of paper towels.
, Melt 1 tbsp. of butter in a microwave safe covered dish for 30 seconds and combine it with 1 tbsp. of olive oil until the ingredients are thoroughly mixed.
 Drizzle the juice of 1 juiced fresh lemon over the fillets.
 Sprinkle the fillets with 2 cloves of minced garlic and sea salt and pepper to taste.
 Bake the tilapia until it's white and opaque and the flesh flakes when it's tested with a fork. Then, remove the tilapia from the oven.
 Sprinkle the tilapia with 2 tbsp. of chopped parsley and serve it with an additional lemon wedge for garnish. Enjoy this tasty dish while it's warm.
