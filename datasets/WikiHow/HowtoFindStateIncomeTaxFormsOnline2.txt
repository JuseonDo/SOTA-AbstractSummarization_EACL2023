 Most states provide printable forms for personal income tax filing on the website of the Department of Revenue or comparable agency., You might want to look at forms from previous years or previous types of forms to analyze changes or corrections. Use your favorite search engine and search by your state and the form number.


For example, if you live in California, the state income tax form is labeled "Form 540."You can look up this form by searching for "California tax form 540" and you should get what you need.
 This could be information such as:


Form instructions;
Current interest rates;
Tax rates;
Due date; and
Tax tables.
