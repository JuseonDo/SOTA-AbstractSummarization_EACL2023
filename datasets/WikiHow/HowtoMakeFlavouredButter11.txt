@summary
mix butter with 1 teaspoon of cinnamon, a pinch of cloves and / or nutmeg or 1 & 1/2 teaspoon of mixed spice or allspice.
@article
 Add 1.5 tablespoons of sweetener, such as icing sugar, honey, maple syrup, golden syrup or brown sugar and mix until combined. Ideally leave overnight before use.


This is delicious spread onto hot baked good such as muffins, toast, cakes, waffles and pancakes.
