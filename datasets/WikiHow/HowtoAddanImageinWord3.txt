@article
 You can do this with images on your computer or images from websites. To copy an image from a website, right-click and select Copy Image. To copy an image on your computer, select it and select Copy from the Edit menu, right-click on it and select Copy, or press Ctrl + C.
 Select Paste from the Edit menu, press Ctrl + V, or right-click and select Paste. Your image will appear where the cursor is.
